THE ANCESTORS

GODDESS

Black woman, you pick your head up Don’t you ever bow
Black woman you are the source of the worlds magic and they can’t replace it!!!
Black woman, your track record is in your blood and that is why they try to erase it .
Black woman, they will taint your beauty but promote it and sell to everyone else
Black woman, they will hate you because your greatest power is the Confidence you have in yourself
BLACK WOMAN, YOU ARE POWERFUL You are a GODDESS from the generation of strong women
BLACK WOMAN, DO NOT QUAVER IN FEAR WE YOUR ANCESTORS ARE ALWAYS WITH YOU!.
